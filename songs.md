# About the songs

The songs in the game come from multiple sources:

- Rolling Stone's The 500 Greatest Songs of All Time (2024 update)
- Spotify's All Out 2010s, 2000s, 1990s

As a target, I aim for the same number of songs per decade and per year.  
The rough target is 150 songs per decade.

No song should appear twice, even if it's a cover.

If I need to remove a song, I try to keep at most two songs per artist.  
I can allow one more if the artist appears as a feature.
